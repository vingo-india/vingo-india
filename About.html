A WORD ABOUT VINGO

Established in the year 2015, A.R. Creations launched brand named “VINGO” in Market. A.R. Creation came in the market with the aspiration of providing unique and international quality clothing lifestyle to every Indian and this thought alone gives it the impetus in going ahead and consistently raising the standards of industrial practices and innovations. The company policy has always been to provide international quality to consumers at Indian prices.​ Starting from Ludhiana, A.R. Creation has surpassed customers’ expectations, ever since it’s inception, which is attributed to its dedicated and disciplined employees base and distribution network across Indian sub.Now A. R. Creations is evolving into the whole new concept, “wings of happiness & Comfort,” a revolution in the context of clothing and dressing. Clothes have been our companion in exploring the outside world. And there comes ‘clothing for life’ which has been our root thinking. Hence, we come with best innovative technology in clothing that accompanies you in your everyday outing to make it lively and fun.​ Today A.R. Creations primarily operates in : Thermal wear, Active wear, Sweatshirts, Jackets for men and women.

ANUJ JAIN(FOUNDER)

Mr. Anuj Jain, a visionary entrepreneur started garment business in 2016 ​ ​ Brand “Vingo” was registered for selling knitted garments in the year 2017. ​ ​ He believes in quality & value for money & takes it as mantra for success. Holding 18 Years of rich experience in reputed companies like NEVA, NAHAR GROUP, AVON CYCLES, THE YAMUNA SYNDICATE as a Brand head.​ ​ Self evaluation is one of his qualities. He is professionally engaged in the analysis, compilation and execution of various types of works in this industry. Innovation is his passion; Conceptual and creative designing is one of his other passions. Apart from this he is involving in customer relation, Project planning & deployment. His top concern is produce work that is orderly and attractive with quality. His strength is ability to understand any topic with the basic common sense. Adopting challenging assignments as well as environment.

REENA JAIN(CFO)

Ms Reena Jain, CFO of A.R. Creations, holds a graduate degree in commerce from Kurukshetra University. She has also taken up various training program from time to time. She has got a hands on expertise of business since year 2000 under the able guidance of her father, Sushil Jain, founder of Noopur Enterprises, Ambala Cantt. Ms Reena Jain has provided outstanding leadership & has contributed in product development planning, strategic decisions, risk management, market research. The Organisation has made good & consistent progress with her insights & has brought the fresh & new generation approach. Her main focus is in providing best consumer service as she believes, “Good Service Is Good Business”.
